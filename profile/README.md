# HES-SO (Haute école spécialisée de Suisse occidentale)

## About

The HES-SO is a Swiss university of applied sciences that offers a wide range of programs in various fields, including engineering, business, health, and social work.
The HES-SO is known for its strong emphasis on practical training and its close ties to industry, which allows students to gain valuable hands-on experience and prepare for successful careers.
The HES-SO has campuses in several cities across western Switzerland, including Geneva, Lausanne, and Fribourg.
